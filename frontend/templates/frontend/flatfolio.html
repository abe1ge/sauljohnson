Flatfolio

One of my gripes with most PHP portfolios is that they either use SQL databases where I don't think they're strictly necessary. If you want art students to have an easy time using your portfolio framework (assuming they're not also developers of course) then why not give them something fast, fluid and filesystem-based?

This is nothing next-generation, but it will show off albums full of artwork with no problem at all, and without a single line of SQL needed to do it.
