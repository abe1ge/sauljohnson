VeryRSS

Written to power my very old blog (which was written in ASP.NET C#) VeryRSS was intended to accompany another couple of libraries on a project that never happened. Essentially just a collection of .NET classes clumsily and hastily implemented, this thing will do the job of allowing you to create and post to an RSS 2.0 feed.
