Greetings

I'm Saul Johnson, a web developer, Windows programmer and all-round language nut who enjoys nothing more of a weekend than to pick up a new programming language over a mug of hot chocolate and perhaps a biscuit or two. I make it my business to sample new programming languages and frameworks as they emerge and run with them as they grow.

Fork, fork, fork

"Come and sample a fresh scoop of my GitHub."

...is just one of the things I might say if I were looking for a way to include this badass picture of Octocat serving ice-cream on my homepage without it seeming out of context.

Seriously though, I have a few projects on there (mainly in Java and C#) that might be worth checking out, especially if you're into peer-to-peer networking (Denobo) or ancient web standards like RSS 2.0 (VeryRSS). The way I see it, the more pairs of eyes that look over my code, the better we as the open-source community have the chance to make it.

So join me over there, fork if you want to work on it, star if you like it, don't star if you don't like it or even just clone it and use it. The more the merrier!

Don't be a stranger!

I maintain a blog called C-flat and I'm on LinkedIn. In case you want to contact me personally, you can get in touch via my contact page. I do not use my Facebook and Google+ accounts for anything aside from family and friends.
