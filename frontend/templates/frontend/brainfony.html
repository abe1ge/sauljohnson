Brainfony

Whoa, whoa, whoa...

Before you get all up in my grill about this page, please relax. Symfony2 is a very well-architected framework with a whole lot of awesome people working on it. It's just that sometimes it makes me want to stick my head in the water cooler. Also this page contains bad language, ye be warned.

There have been times when I've been working with Symfony2 when I've literally spent all day trying to determine which of my bundles contains the controller that renders a template for a sub-request in a Twig file nested 15 levels deep. It's at these points that I find myself longing for something else. Anything else. Even if that something slows my progress to a crawl, I'll at least be moving forward.

This is where Brainfony comes in. Now I can code for the web in Brainfuck, a programming language with an unbelievable amount of untapped power ready to be unleashed into the wonderful world of web development. I'm not going to document the language here, but I will provide a hello world program so you can see just how sadistic a Turing-complete language can get. I hope this will pique your curiosity and get you to look at the delightful page Wikipedia has on the subject.
